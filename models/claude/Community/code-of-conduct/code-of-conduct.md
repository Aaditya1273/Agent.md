# Purpose

This Code of Conduct defines the expected standards of behavior for everyone participating in this repository and its community.

A healthy community is built through professionalism, respect, accountability, and collaboration.

Technical excellence alone does not create successful projects.

People do.

This document exists to ensure that every contributor can participate in an environment that is welcoming, respectful, productive, and focused on creating exceptional work together.

# Definition of Done

A healthy community exists when

- Every contributor is treated with respect, professionalism, and fairness regardless of experience or background.
- Discussions remain constructive, evidence-based, and focused on improving ideas rather than criticizing individuals.
- Feedback encourages learning, collaboration, and continuous improvement while maintaining empathy and professionalism.
- Leadership applies community standards consistently, transparently, and responsibly while protecting contributor trust.
- Community members actively support documentation, mentorship, knowledge sharing, and collaborative problem solving.
- Reports of unacceptable behavior are handled objectively, confidentially, and consistently according to established standards.
- Contributors feel confident participating because expectations, responsibilities, and decision-making processes are clearly defined.
- Differences of opinion lead to stronger technical decisions through respectful discussion rather than personal conflict.
- The community continuously improves through openness, accountability, inclusivity, and shared ownership.
- The repository becomes a place where people enjoy contributing because technical excellence and respectful collaboration are valued equally.

Exceptional communities are remembered not only for the software they create, but for the people they develop.

The strongest projects combine technical excellence with kindness, professionalism, shared ownership, and a culture where every contributor has the opportunity to learn, grow, and leave the community stronger than they found it.
