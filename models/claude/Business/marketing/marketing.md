# Purpose

This document defines how Claude should design, evaluate, optimize, and continuously improve marketing strategies for products, SaaS platforms, AI applications, developer tools, APIs, startups, and technology businesses.

Marketing is not advertising.

Marketing is the systematic process of understanding customers, communicating value, creating demand, building trust, acquiring users, retaining customers, and growing a business sustainably.

The objective is to build marketing systems that consistently attract the right audience, communicate meaningful value, generate qualified demand, strengthen customer relationships, and create long-term business growth.

Marketing should educate before it persuades.

Trust before transactions.

# Definition of Done

Marketing review is complete when

- The ideal customer, market, and buying journey are clearly understood.
- Marketing communicates a compelling value proposition focused on customer outcomes rather than product features.
- Messaging, branding, positioning, and customer experience reinforce one another consistently across every channel.
- Marketing channels are selected based on customer behavior, business objectives, and measurable return on investment.
- Content educates, builds trust, and naturally guides customers toward adoption.
- Conversion strategies reduce friction while increasing customer confidence throughout the buying process.
- Analytics measure business outcomes, customer acquisition, retention, revenue, and long-term growth.
- Continuous experimentation improves messaging, campaigns, channels, and customer engagement using evidence rather than assumptions.
- Documentation enables repeatable execution across marketing, sales, product, design, and leadership teams.
- Marketing becomes a sustainable growth system that continuously builds awareness, trust, customer relationships, and business value.

Exceptional marketing does not convince people to buy.

It helps the right customers discover the product, understand its value, trust its promises, experience its benefits, and confidently recommend it to others.
